    • Green Onion Pancake

      When we were stuck at home during the beginning of the COVID quarantine, we were very limited in the supplies we had for cooking. We couldn't find yeast anywhere. This recipe was great because it required few ingredients. Added bonus: if you place the stems of the green onions into a cup of water, it will grow fairly quickly, making it a renewable resource!

    Ingredients

      3 cups flour
      1 cup hot water
      1/2 cup water
      Butter
      Salt
      1 to 2 bunches of green onions

    Instructions

      Put 3 cups of flour into a bowl, then add hot water.
      Mix thoroughly and add 1/2 cup of cold water saving a little.
      Roll into a ball and add rest of cold water to get excess flour into the ball of dough.
      Roll out dough into flat sheet, about 1/8 inch thick.
      Spread butter over dough and add green onions and salt.
      Roll up into a log and grab a handful.
      Roll it into a ball and press flat.
      Pan fry until both sides are browned.

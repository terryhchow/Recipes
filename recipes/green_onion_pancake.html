Green Onion Pancake

Ingredients

    3 cups flour
    1 cup hot water
    1/2 cup water
    Butter
    Salt
    1 to 2 bunches of green onions

Instructions

    Put 3 cups of flour into a bowl, then add hot water.
    Mix thoroughly and add 1/2 cup of cold water saving a little.
    Roll into a ball and add rest of cold water to get excess flour into the ball of dough.
    Roll out dough into flat sheet, about 1/8 inch thick.
    Spread butter over dough and add green onions and salt.
    Roll up into a log and grab a handful.
    Roll it into a ball and press flat.
    Fry to desire.

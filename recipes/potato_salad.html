Potato Salad

Ingredients

    2 lbs small red potatoes scrubbed and quartered
    2 hard boiled eggs chopped
    1 lbs bacon (give or take)
    6 scallions chopped
    1 red onion chopped
    3/4 cups of mayonnaise
    3 tbsp whole-grain mustard
    2 tbsp red wine vinegar
    salt & pepper

Instructions

    Cut potatoes into 1/2 inch pieces and boil from cold water until tender (15-20 mins).
    Test with a fork to see when it easily breaks apart for doneness.
    Drain potatoes but DO NOT rinse with cold water. (Note: this is actually supposed to be a warm potato salad, but it's good cold too). It says to cool on a sheet pan, but I usually leave it in the pot.
    Trim rind and cook bacon. Personally, I like to use the oven. Place the cooked bacon on a paper towel to drain the grease.
    Boil Eggs
    In a separate bowl, mix together mayonnaise, mustard, red wine vinegar, scallions, onion, salt, and pepper.
    Add everything into a large bowl, mix together and enjoy.

    • Bacon Potato Salad

      This recipe is a favourite of Jeff's friends and the perfect addition to any barbeque. It is also the reason for the creation of this website. One day, Jeff's friend asked for this recipe. Seeing him type it out to share via email, I thought that there could be a better way for us to share our recipes. This potato salad is loaded with so much bacon that Jeff's friend Andrew calls it "Bacon Salad."

    Ingredients

      2 lbs small red potatoes scrubbed and quartered
      2 hard boiled eggs chopped
      1 lbs bacon
      6 scallions chopped
      1 red onion chopped
      3/4 cups of mayonnaise
      3 tbsp whole-grain mustard
      2 tbsp red wine vinegar
      salt & pepper

    Instructions

      Cut potatoes into 1/2 inch pieces and boil from cold water until tender (15-20 mins).
      Test with a fork to see when it easily breaks apart for doneness.
      Drain potatoes but DO NOT rinse with cold water. (Note: this is actually supposed to be a warm potato salad, but it's good cold too).
      It says to cool on a sheet pan, but I usually leave it in the pot.
      Trim rind and cook bacon. Personally, I like to use the oven. Place the cooked bacon on a paper towel to drain the grease.
      Boil Eggs
      In a separate bowl, mix together mayonnaise, mustard, red wine vinegar, scallions, onion, salt, and pepper.
      Add everything into a large bowl, mix together and enjoy.

    • Chinese Hot Pot

      Hot Pot is great for when it is cold, or when I am too lazy to cook something. I just have to make a simple broth, and we cook everything else by the table! Here are some tips on how to put together a hot pot spread.

    Soup

      For soup, I will usually just do a carton of chicken broth, boiled with either daikon radish or carrots. If I want to go fancier, I will make a Chinese herbal soup for the base. Here's where I buy my Chinese herbal mixes, my favorite is the Health & Immunity Mix.

    Ingredients

      Napa cabbage, chopped
      Baby bok choy
      Enoki mushrooms
      King trumpet mushrooms, sliced
      Tofu, cubed
      Fried gluten balls
      Fish balls, especially the ones filled with roe
      Beef balls
      Sliced beef
      Sliced pork
      Frozen udon

Salted Caramel Cupcakes

    Yields 12 cupcakes

Ingredients

Cupcakes

    1 1/2 cups (190g) all-purpose flour
    3/4 teaspoon baking powder
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    1 cup (200g) packed dark brown sugar
    1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar
    1 stick unsalted butter at room temperature
    2 eggs at room temperature
    1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    1/2 cup whole milk or buttermilk or half-half

Salted Caramel Sauce

    2 cups granulated sugar
    12 tablespoons unsalted butter at room temperature cut into pieces
    1 cup of heavy cream at room temperature
    1 tablespoon fleur de sel or other flaky sea salt

Frosting

    1 stick of unsalted butter at room temperature
    1 cup (200g) packed dark brown sugar
    1/3 cup (80ml) heavy cream
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    2-3 cups (240-360g) powdered sugar sifted

Instructions

    Preheat oven to 350F and line a 12 cup muffin pan with paper liners
    In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. In a separate medium bowl, beat the butter and sugars together at a medium high speed until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, vanilla, and milk, then mix until no flour remains. Do not overmix
    Fill each cupcake liner 1/2 way full with batter. Bake cupcakes for 240-360 minutes or until an inserted toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean. Let the cupcakes cool before frosting.
    For the caramel sauce, add sugar to a saugepan and whisk as it melts. Stop whisking once it's melted and only swirl the pot occasionally (do not stir). Continue cooking until it reaches a deep amber color (approximately 350F). Once it reaches the dark amber color, add butter immediately and whisk until dissolved. WARNING: Caramel will bubble up when adding the butter. Remove from heat, and slowly add cream. WARNING: Caramel will bubble up when adding cream. Once the cream has been incorporated and a smooth sauce is formed, add fleur de sel and whisk to incorporate. Let it cool 10-15 minutes prior to storing or using.
